About Trip Budget Manager

We are a passionate team of travelers and developers dedicated to making financial planning for trips as breathtaking as the destinations themselves. Our tool simplifies complex budget tracking into an intuitive, visual experience, ensuring you focus on the memories, not the math.

Terms & Conditions

By using the Trip Budget Manager, you agree that all budget calculations are estimates. We do not store your personal ID data (Passport/Aadhar) on any server; all data remains local to your current browser session for privacy. Please travel responsibly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?
Absolutely! The Trip Budget Manager is completely free for all users to help you plan your next adventure.
Is my personal data safe?
Yes. We prioritize privacy. All budget data and your login hash are saved **locally to your browser's storage** and are not sent to any external server.
